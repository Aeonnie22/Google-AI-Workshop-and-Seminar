Finance / Liquidation Protocol

To process clean refunds and payments for suppliers, ensure compliance with the following university directives:

  1. Always request a valid BIR-registered Official Receipt (OR) for purchases exceeding ₱1,000.
  2. Disbursements completed via GCash/Maya must have screenshot confirmation containing the 12 or 13-digit Reference Code.
  3. Consolidate weekly logs in Excel under designated accounts before submitting to the Executive Board Auditor.
  4. Never log personal items on the organizational budget; audited items require physical inventory verification.
📁

University Logistics & Venue Sourcing

Booking guidelines for university facilities (e.g. Amphitheater, Multi-purpose Sports Center, Classrooms):

  1. Obtain the official Physical Facilities Office (PFO) reservation form.
  2. Collect safety signatures from both the faculty adviser and the College Dean.
  3. Submit documents to PFO at least 10 working days prior to the event date.
  4. Coordinate with electricity services if extra outlets, projectors, or speaker systems are required.
📁

Marketing & Graphic Design Publication Policies

Guidance to maintain visual cohesion across social media profiles and print media:

  1. Always employ the official brand fonts (Outfit and Space Grotesk).
  2. Include the official organization transparent watermark in the upper right corner of all pubmats.
  3. Ensure alternative text descriptions (alt text) are attached to Facebook and Instagram publications for accessibility.
  4. Publicity materials mentioning university partners require explicit written sign-off from the Sponsorship Committee.
